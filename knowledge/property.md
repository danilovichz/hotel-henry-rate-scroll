## Rate Structure

### Two Rate Columns (per the Rate Shop)

| Column | What It Is | Who Sees It |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| IHG rate | Rate on holidayinn.com / IHG corporate site | IHG loyalty members, direct bookers (~9% cheaper) |
| Expedia rate | Rate on Expedia / public OTAs | Most guests |

IHG direct saves the hotel ~15% Expedia commission, but the 12% IHG franchise fee still applies.

### Cost Per Booking Dollar

| Channel | Cost |
|---------|------|
| Expedia booking | $0.15 (Expedia) + $0.12 (IHG brand) = **$0.27 gone before ops** |
| IHG direct | $0.09–$0.12 (IHG only) — significantly better margin |

**Goal:** Push bookings toward IHG direct whenever possible.
